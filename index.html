About Emily

I have been a problem-solver with a knack for investigation my entire life. When I fell into coding, I felt like I had finally found my place in the world. I also am consumed by wanderlust, and I can be sparked with inspiration by the new places I visit and people I meet! I am a huge fan of how coding is a pliable and usable art. If we can think it, we can make it real! I strive to work on a team of great programmers. I feel it is one of the most rewarding elements of this industry. When we combine brainpower, experience, and creativity, the possibilities are endless! My hobbies outside of coding include: DJing on my Pioneer XDJ-RX2 mixer, reading, and longboarding.

I can (want to) provide clients with these services:

  1. Custom software engineering
  2. Website and mobile app creation
  3. Turning ideas and dreams into a final product
